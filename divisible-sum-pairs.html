Divisible Sum PairsEasy

Given an array of integers and a positive integer \(k\), determine the number of \((i, j)\) pairs where \(i < j\) and \((ar[i] + ar[j])\) is divisible by \(k\).

Example

\(ar = [1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6]\)

\(k = 5\)

Three pairs meet the criteria: \((1, 4), (2, 3), (4, 6)\).
